How To Use

If you (the player), move, attack or changes your armour, your turn will be spent and until, the monsters have moved or attacked.  The objective is to work your way down the floors to destroy the king who is positioned on the bottom floor.



Up arrow:           move up

down arrow:       move down

Left arrow:         move left

Right arrow:       move right



Attacks

Space:    basic attack (low amount of damage)

q:           water trident (effective against fire enemies)

w:          fire staff (effective against bugs)

e:           poisonous spear (effective against fast enemies)

r:            sword (effective for damaging multiple enemies at once)

t:            bow (effective against low armoured enemies from a distance)

y:           axe (effective against skeletons)

u:           magic (effective for fighting demons)

i:            legend (needed to defeat boss)

   



Defence

1:           basic armour (low protection against basic enemies)

2:           water robe (effective at defending against fire enemies)

3:           fire robe (effective at defending against bugs)

4:           poison armour (gives enemies poisoning if they attack you while you are wearing the armour)

5:           steel armour (high efficiency at defending against multiple enemies at once)

6:           light armour (great for sprinting two tiles)

7:           leather armour (great at defending all around attacks)

8:           magic robe (low efficiency at defence but boosts attack)

9:           legend armour (needed for defeating the boss)
